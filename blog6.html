How to Maximize Your Chances

  • Start early: Most scholarships require applications 6-12 months in advance. Begin preparing now.
  • Get strong recommendation letters: Ask professors or employers who know you well. Provide them with your CV and personal statement.
  • Write a compelling personal statement: Tell your unique story. Connect your experiences to your goals and how you'll contribute to Liberia's development.
  • Meet language requirements: Take IELTS, TOEFL, or other required tests early. Aim for scores above minimum requirements.
  • Apply to multiple scholarships: Don't put all your eggs in one basket. Apply to 5-10 scholarships to increase your chances.
  • Seek professional guidance: Our team at Scholarship Hub can review your applications and provide personalized advice.

Application Checklist

  • ✓ Valid passport (ensure it has at least 2 years validity)
  • ✓ Academic transcripts and certificates (certified copies)
  • ✓ Language proficiency scores (IELTS, TOEFL, etc.)
  • ✓ Statement of Purpose / Personal Essay
  • ✓ Letters of recommendation (2-3)
  • ✓ CV/Resume tailored to the scholarship
  • ✓ Research proposal (for PhD applications)
  • ✓ Proof of citizenship (Liberian passport or national ID)
  • ✓ Completed application forms
