How to Maximize Your Chances

  • Start early: Most scholarships require applications 6-12 months in advance.
  • Get strong recommendation letters: Ask professors or employers who know you well.
  • Write a compelling personal statement: Tell your unique story and connect it to Liberia's development.
  • Meet language requirements: Take IELTS/TOEFL early. Aim for scores above minimums.
  • Apply to multiple scholarships: Apply to 5-10 scholarships to increase your chances.
