🔍 How to Verify Any Immigration Consultant

  • Canada: ICCRC register at iccrc-crcic.ca - must show "Active" status
  • Australia: MARA register at mara.gov.au - valid 7-digit MARN required
  • United Kingdom: IAA register at gov.uk/find-an-immigration-adviser
  • USA: DOJ Recognition at justice.gov/eoir - recognized organizations only
  • ⚠️ NEVER pay for a job offer - legitimate employers never charge for job placement
  • ⚠️ If someone says "the website is not updated" - that is a lie. Walk away immediately.
