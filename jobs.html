Global Immigration Resources

Official government portals and verified immigration information for African applicants — founded 2023, trusted across Africa.

CRITICAL WARNING: Always use official government websites for visa applications. Legitimate immigration processes never require payment for job offers or guaranteed visas. NEVER pay for a job offer.

How to Verify Official Immigration Portals

  • Always use .gov domains for official government immigration websites
  • Canada: CICC register at iccrc-crcic.ca for consultant verification
  • Australia: MARA register at mara.gov.au - valid 7-digit MARN required
  • UK: IAA register at gov.uk/find-an-immigration-adviser
  • USA: DOJ Recognition at justice.gov/eoir for recognized organizations
  • NEVER pay for a job offer - legitimate employers and government portals never charge for job placement
  • If someone says "the website is not updated" - that is a lie. Walk away immediately.
