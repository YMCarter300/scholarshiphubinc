Cost of Living: 10 Most Affordable Cities for Students

March 28, 2026 11 min read By LinkAfric Team #CostOfLiving #AffordableCities #StudentBudget

Studying abroad is a life-changing experience, but the cost of living can make or break your budget. The good news? There are incredible cities around the world where you can live comfortably on a student budget without sacrificing quality of life. LinkAfric Inc. (founded 2023) has analyzed global data to bring you the top 10 most affordable student cities.

Key Insight: The difference between the most expensive and most affordable student cities can be over $2,000 per month. Choosing the right city can save you $20,000+ over a 3-year degree program.

Essential Budgeting Apps: Splitwise (split bills with roommates), Too Good To Go (discounted food), and local transport apps can save you hundreds each month.
